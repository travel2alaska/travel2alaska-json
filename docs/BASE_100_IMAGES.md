# The Base 100 Image Inventory

To launch and effectively scale an Alaska travel site without it feeling generic or relying heavily on repetitive placeholders, you need exactly 100 highly specific, premium core images. 

By categorizing them against our taxonomy (Regions, Destinations, Activities, Wildlife, Transit, and Culture), we can build an automated mapping engine that falls back to these high-quality assets when a specific local listing is missing a photo.

---

## 1. Main Geographic Regions (5)
*These act as category headers and massive hero images for top-level navigation.*
1. **Southcentral**: Dramatic mountains meeting the ocean (e.g., Turnagain Arm).
2. **Southeast**: Deep green temperate rainforests, misty fjords, and totems.
3. **Interior**: Endless taiga, golden hour light, vast rivers.
4. **Arctic**: Treeless tundra, permafrost, ocean ice, midnight sun.
5. **Southwest**: Volcanic landscapes, brown bears, rugged Aleutian coastlines.
